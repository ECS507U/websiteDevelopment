About Us

This website is dedicated to those people who are seeking for making friends and relationships using the internet. We know that it might me hard for people with autism to make friends and we hope that our website will help autistic people to meet new people in a fun and interactive way.

We have been working really hard to make sure this website is suitable for people users from all spectrums and all genders. Although the website is targeted at autistic users, it can be used by any people wishing to make friends. If you have any questions or suggestions on how to improve our website, please do not hesitate to contact us by clicking on the following link : Contact Us
